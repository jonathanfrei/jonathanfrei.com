The right way to make a website

Just make the content in a notepad, and push it out onto the internet. Everything can fit into a single file. I can link to whatever I want and add the images in line or through hot linking as needed.

Is this a better way to work on the web? Probably not, but it is fun. It's like driving a stick shift...getting you closer to the metal.

Making websites work makes me feel like this.
